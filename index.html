ABOUT ME

I don't need workout. I overfit.

I am a third year Bachelors student in Computer Science. I spent my last summer at TU Braunschweig working under Professor Sandor Fekete on approximation algorithms for shortest path planning in Swarm Robotics. Having taken courses on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I worked on a Tetris playing agent as a part of my course project on AI, where I used Genetic Algorithms and Particle Swarm Optimization to obtain generic weights for a linearly modeled evaluation function. I have also been offered a research position at Samsung Korea in Seoul for Summer 2018.
